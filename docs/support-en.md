## Troubleshooting

**Subscriptions disappeared after reinstalling the app**
: Subctrl stores every record locally. Make sure iCloud/device backups are enabled before deleting the app. If you already removed it, there is no remote copy to restore.

**Notifications stopped firing**
: Open iOS Settings > Notifications > Subctrl and re-enable alerts. Then open the Support tab inside the app to re-register reminders.
